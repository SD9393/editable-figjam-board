## Troubleshooting

**Q: I don't see updates from teammates**  
A: Refresh your browser. Check your internet connection.

**Q: How do I change my name?**  
A: Clear your browser cache or use incognito mode, then re-enter your name.

**Q: Can I undo a change?**  
A: Currently no - changes are immediate. Be careful with deletes!

**Q: What if two people edit at once?**  
A: Last save wins. Changes sync in milliseconds so conflicts are rare.
